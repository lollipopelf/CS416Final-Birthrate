Economic Boom, Fewer Babies?

China’s GDP has grown steadily over the past two decades. But strangely, the number of newborns has dropped every year since 2016.

Visualizing the Trend

This chart compares GDP growth and birthrate from 2000 to 2023.

Why is Birthrate Falling?

  • 🏙️ Urban pressure: Housing & education costs are high
  • 🎓 Women pursue higher education → later marriage
  • 🧠 New generation mindset: Many don’t want kids
  • 📉 Policy impact is limited
  • 🦠 Pandemic (2020–2022)

Data source: World Bank, China Statistical Yearbook
